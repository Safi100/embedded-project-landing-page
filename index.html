With a fingerprint sensor, keypad, and LCD display, our system offers a comprehensive authentication solution. Users can easily verify their identity through fingerprint scans or keypad input, with real-time feedback displayed on the LCD. Upon successful authentication, the system triggers predefined actions, such as unlocking doors or executing specific tasks. This integration of hardware components ensures both security and user convenience in every interaction.
